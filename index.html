About Me

Hello! I'm Hyma Aishwarya.

Welcome to the personal page of an aspiring data analyst and BTech CSE-AI&ML student, where passion for data meets a flair for creativity. With a strong foundation in artificial intelligence and machine learning, I am dedicated to exploring the depths of data analysis and its applications. My academic pursuits are enriched by my enthusiasm for research and data processing, which drive me to uncover insights and forge innovative solutions. Beyond the realm of numbers and algorithms, I find joy in the art of cooking, the rhythm of dancing, and the worlds unveiled through reading. These interests not only provide a well-deserved respite from my studies but also fuel my creativity, allowing me to approach analytical challenges with a fresh perspective. My portfolio includes hands-on projects like developing a web-based movie recommendation system using Python, reflecting my ability to translate complex concepts into tangible outcomes. Join me on this journey as I navigate the ever-evolving landscape of AI/ML, one data set at a time.
